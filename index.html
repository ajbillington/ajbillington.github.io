Interior

Holographic Mid-Air Display System - An exceptional interior designed for the ultimate comfort and practicality.A high-end advanced interior design with a touch of modern luxury. Customised Dashboard - Completely customizable with endless color and material combinations, accommodating a unique steering wheel, digital cluster and holographic display. The exclusive modern seats of the Lykan are developed for ultimate comfort and safety. Designed with a wrap around carbon fiber body shell, keeping the driver’s body and neck stable when driving at high speeds.

Technology

The first holographic display system with interactive motion, giving the driver and passenger full interaction with the multimedia interface of the Lykan as well as other vehicle control systems. The Lykan is simply years ahead in innovation and exclusivity. A unique customizable performance screen is designed to give quick access to key driving information, from several engine performance and efficiency parameters. When virtual meets reality.

Top Speed
And Price

It's top speed is at an impressive 385 km/h (240 mph) although it is depedent on the gear ratios. The insane acceleration is at 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) in 2.8 seconds while 0-200 km/h (0-125 mph) is at 9.4 seconds. This car will take a lot out of your wallet. It is sold at 3.4 million US dollars. The car is 3rd most expensive car in the world behind the Maybach Exelero (8 million USD) and the Lamborghini Veneno (4.5 million USD) There have only ever been 7 cars released.
